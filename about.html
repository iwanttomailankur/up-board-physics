About this site

A study companion for UP Board students.

This site organizes Class 12 Physics into the exact unit structure used by the Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad (UPMSP), so students can move through the syllabus in the same order and weightage they'll see on the exam paper.

What's here

Each unit links to a short video explainer covering the core concepts, formulas, and common exam questions for that topic — built to sit alongside your NCERT textbook, not replace it.

Who's behind it

This project is run independently as a study resource and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to UPMSP or the Government of Uttar Pradesh. Syllabus references are drawn from publicly available UPMSP materials.
